MagicalMind

Rules

The goal is to find the correct combination of symbols, that is randomly set when you press the "New Game" button.
To win, you must input the correct key before you run out of tries.
The Hogwarts Crest Symbol, with the regular colors, will tell you how many pieces you have in the right position.
The Hogwarts Crest Symbol, with the strange colors, will tell you how many pieces of your code belog to the key, but are misplaced.
